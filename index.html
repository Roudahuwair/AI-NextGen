Myths vs Facts

Myth: AI will take all jobs

Fact: Tasks change and new roles appear. Humans with smart tools are more valuable.

Myth: AI is only for experts

Fact: Easy tools exist for students, creators, and entrepreneurs.

Myth: AI is always dangerous

Fact: Responsible use and ethics frameworks reduce risks and increase benefits.

Myth: AI kills creativity

Fact: It unlocks new experimentation and frees time for bigger ideas.

FAQ

How do I start learning?

Begin with computational thinking and data basics, then try no-code tools.

Do I need advanced math?

Not for everyday use. Specialized paths need math and probability over time.

How do I use it safely?

Verify sources, respect privacy, and review your platform’s data policies.
